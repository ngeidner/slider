The Cost of Being a Woman
I am playing around with the idea of using Juxtapose.JS from the Knight Lab as a simple, interactive graphic form. I created this page as a proof of concept before creating an assignment for students in my data journalism course. It use 2013 income data from the U.S. Census. I am still not sure Juxtapose is a good way to do this, but I think there could be something to the act revealing information that could be beneficial for the user.
